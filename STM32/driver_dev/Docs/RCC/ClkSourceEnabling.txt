




-> PLL Enabling:

               1. Enable PLL source (HSI or HSE)
                           ↓
                2. Wait until source is ready
                           ↓
                3. Disable PLL (if it was already enabled)
                           ↓
                4. Configure PLLCFGR (PLLM, PLLN, PLLP, PLLQ, Source)
                           ↓
                5. Enable PLL
                           ↓
                6. Wait for PLLRDY
                           ↓
                7. Switch SYSCLK to PLL
                           ↓
                8. Wait until SWS = PLL
-> HSE Enabling:
             
                1. Enable HSE (CR register:HSEON)

                2. Wait till HSE is locked (enabled and stable | HSERDY)

-> HSI Enabling:
             
                1. Enable HSI (CR register:HSION)

                2. Wait till HSI is locked (enabled and stable | HSIRDY)



For switching clock source after Enabling the source (HSI/HSE/PLL), 

              - check if source is READY (again for safety)
              - write into SW bits for selection
              - wait till SWS becomes clock source bits( eg: wait till SWS == 0 for HSI selection)
